Game Design Document



1\. Overview



●Game Title: Heist the Gallery



●Genre: 2D Stealth–Puzzle Game



●Target Audience: Children



●Summary:



The game is a 2D cartoon-style stealth puzzle game where the main character tries to steal valuable paintings from an art gallery due to financial struggles. Players must avoid security cameras, guards, and laser systems while solving puzzles to escape without getting caught.



●Key Features:

\- Stealth-based gameplay with security cameras, guards, and laser traps

\- 2D cartoon-style art in a detailed art gallery environment

\- Dynamic hiding system using objects

\- Alarm system that increases difficulty when detected



2\. Game Mechanics



●Core Gameplay: The player controls a thief who moves through an art gallery to steal valuable paintings. The main objective is to avoid detection by security cameras, guards, and laser systems. Players can hide behind objects, disable security systems, and solve environmental puzzles to open new paths. Each level requires careful timing, observation, and strategic movement to successfully escape with the stolen artwork.



●Controls:

\- \*\*A-D / Arrow Keys\*\* – Move character

\- \*\*Q\*\* – Switch character

\- \*\*E\*\* – Interact with objects

\- \*\*E\*\* – Hide / Unhide behind objects

\- \*\*Space\*\* – Jump

\- \*\*Esc\*\* – Pause menu



●Characters:

Player – The Thief

\- Can move, hide behind objects, and interact with the environment

\- Able to disable security panels and collect valuable paintings

\- Limited stamina when sprinting to encourage strategic movement



NPCs



\*\*Security Guards\*\*

\- Patrol predefined paths

\- Can detect the player if they enter their field of view



●Objects:



Paintings



\- Main collectible items in the game

\- Must be stolen to complete level objectives



Security Panels



\- Can be interacted with to disable cameras and lasers temporarily



Hiding Objects



\- Player can hide behind them to avoid detection



Laser Emitters



\- Create laser barriers that trigger the alarm when crossed



●Progression System:



The game is divided into multiple levels that gradually increase in difficulty. Each new level introduces more complex security systems, additional guards, and harder puzzles. Players do not level up through experience points, but instead improve by learning enemy patterns, timing, and stealth strategies. Optional collectible items can unlock bonus challenges and alternate escape routes.



●Difficulty Level:



The game features progressive difficulty that increases naturally with each level. Early stages introduce basic mechanics such as cameras and simple guard patrols, while later levels add faster guards, wider camera vision, more laser traps, and complex puzzles. Difficulty is adjusted by increasing enemy awareness, shorter reaction times, and more limited safe hiding spots.



3\. Story and World



●Story Summary:



The story follows a struggling artist who turns to theft after falling into financial hardship. Desperate to survive, the protagonist breaks into a prestigious art gallery to steal valuable paintings. As the game progresses, the player discovers that the gallery hides secrets about the dark business side of the art world, pushing the character to question their choices and motivations.



●Characters:



The Thief (Main Character)



A struggling artist who lost financial stability after being exploited by wealthy galleries. Motivated by desperation, they break into the art gallery to steal paintings, not only for money but to reclaim control over their own work and dignity. As the story progresses, their internal conflict grows between survival and morality.



Security Guard



A disciplined employee who believes strongly in duty and order. They see the gallery as a symbol of cultural protection, unaware of the corruption behind its operations. This creates a moral contrast between the guard and the thief.



●World:



The game takes place inside a realistic urban art gallery located in a modern city. The gallery features exhibition rooms, private collections, storage areas, and security zones. The main character is a known criminal who enters the gallery with the clear intent of stealing valuable artworks, rather than being morally justified or heroic.



●Atmosphere:



The intended atmosphere is tense and suspenseful, encouraging careful movement and strategic thinking. While the visual style is cartoonish, the mood remains serious, mysterious, and immersive to keep players alert and cautious.



4\. Visuals and Audio



●Art Style:



The game features a 2D cartoon-style visual design with clean outlines, vibrant colors.



●Interface (UI):



The user interface is minimal and clean, displaying only essential elements such as stamina, detection alerts, and objective indicators. The UI is designed to be easy to understand and non-intrusive.



●Music:



The music consists of low-tempo, suspenseful background tracks that intensify during chase or alert moments, enhancing player tension and immersion.



●Sound Effects:



Sound effects include footsteps and alarm sirens.
